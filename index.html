One of the most exciting projects I worked on during my time at UNCG was a Car Seat Adjustment System powered by AI, computer vision, and Arduino. Built an AI-based system to convert handwritten notes into editable digital text. Used Optical Character Recognition (OCR) with deep learning models trained on handwritten datasets. The system leveraged computer vision to detect and segment text regions, then translated the handwriting into machine-readable format. Ideal for digitizing notes, forms, and archival documents with improved accuracy over traditional OCR engines.
